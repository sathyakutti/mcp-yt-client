## üí° Pro Tips

### Finding High-Value Content
- Posts with **100+ engagements** usually have substance
- Look for **verified company pages** (blue checkmark)
- **Articles** (not just posts) have more depth
- Check **comments** - often have valuable insights
- **Polls** show what the community cares about

### Efficient Research
- Open **5-10 posts in tabs** before documenting
- Use **LinkedIn's "Save" feature** to bookmark posts
- Take **screenshots** of key charts/data
- Note **engagement patterns** (what resonates?)

### Avoiding Noise
- Skip promotional posts (product ads)
- Ignore generic "tips and tricks" posts
- Focus on **data-driven content**
- Look for **real-world case studies**
- Prioritize **technical deep-dives**
